About Me

  • I have led an ECommerce QE automation team, architecting multiple frameworks (Playwright, TestCafe, Nightwatch, API) and scaling automated QA from zero to 3,000+ daily regression tests completed in an hour across 96 global websites.
  • I have identified hundreds of software and content defects per year (Jira tickets), significantly enhancing releases, website reliability, and customer satisfaction.
  • I have built additional tools for API testing, SEO change tracking, analytics tag verification, and automated screenshot captures to help development and business teams reduce their repetitive testing needs.
  • I have integrated regression test suites into CircleCI pipeline parallel workflows, enabling both on-demand execution (via API) and scheduled runs (via cron jobs) to ensure that test results are available quickly or by the start of each workday.
  • I have designed and implemented dashboards and Slack alerting systems to provide real-time visibility into test results and quickly surfaced critical issues.
